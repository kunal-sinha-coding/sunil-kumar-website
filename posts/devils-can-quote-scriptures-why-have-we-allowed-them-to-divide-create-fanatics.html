India to Bharat · Essay #159

Devils can quote scriptures – Why have we allowed them to divide & create fanatics?

Purpose of any Religion is only to inspire us to be Good: Why then do followers of religions end up killing, terrorizing, and committing all sorts of harassment, atrocities and communal fights? It cannot be the fault of the founders of religions or the religions themselves! So, it must be the fault of preachers, maulvis, pundits of religions. It is they who must be provoking their simple followers to commit mayhem, carnage, crusades in the name of expanding, saving or protecting their religion or God or punishing those who insult their God or scripture.

Minimum Essentials of a Religion : Founders give their spiritual message to a particular people at a given time in particular circumstances so as to inspire people to be virtuous, follow certain ways of worship to attain ‘God’. However, as time passes situations change and followers many a times miss the “essentials” and hold on to the “non-essentials and supports”. Thus, what remains are the, rituals, symbols, words, mythology, legends, etc. along with notes of ‘teachings’, sacred places and objects. And, the followers and preachers, who may not be spiritually mature, and their misinterpretations of the ‘original’ message.

In these “supports”, Religions are of course different; but in the “essentials”, bereft of all ‘add-on’, all religions simply inspire us to imbibe and follow universal human values in our work and life, as that alone is the critical path to ‘God’; whatever be our various ideas of God or ways of worship.

In the ancient era when communication was extremely limited, certain ideas could be claimed to be uniquely and exclusively ours. However, situation today is extremely open and different and, details of all religions are at our finger tips! Modern education has raised our intellect and so, our spirit of inquiry and reasoning will not accept unreasonable ideas. We live in a global village, and so, narrow, self-centered ideas and superstitions cannot survive, unless preachers force their simple or self-centered flock to believe something, against common sense!

All Scriptures are liable for Interpretations, narrowly or universally: For example, according to Islam, all individuals should lead lives of worship and devotion to God. Similarly, according to Christianity, what is the use of knowing by heart the entire Bible, without having love of God and his blessings! Indeed, a humble farmer or a cobbler, who does his duty is better than a proud scholar who doesn’t fear and love God. Similarly, what use is the knowledge of many things and to know the whole of teachings! Mere words do not satisfy the soul or God; but a virtuous life and a pure conscience gives rest to the mind and love for God.

Again, worship in Islam is discussed variously, fearfulness, humility, love for God, relationship with God of love and fear, hope of receiving his mercy and blessings; essence is God-fearing; pray to God as if you are seeing Him. So, aim of worship is to enter into a relationship with God, and reach God. To find God thus in this life is the highest reality of religion.

Again, the five pillars of Islam are: There is but one God and one Prophet; to offer prayers; to give alms; to perform pilgrimage; to keep fast. All these ‘faith, prayers, charity, pilgrimage, fasting’ are not mere rituals but the ways to receive choicest divine blessings.

One God, many Religions to It: The above are universal ideals and are certainly acceptable to all religions. Why shouldn’t this be a part of instructions for all of us! It must be recognized that in this age when all ideas are an open book and are available for all of us to discuss, debate, reason, question, any narrow, self-centered, limited idea will not be acceptable to people; but it can be used to provoke simple-minded people to act wrongly! Of course, the problem comes when narrow minded preachers’ dwell upon the differences; e.g. male or female individuals; ways of worship; my God, not your God; and then simple devotees keep quarreling with other religions over such trivialities.

After all, the idea of God in all religions is always associated with infinitude, omnipotent, omnipresence, omniscience, and therefore, mathematically, God has to be One and only one. That is how Vivekananda became a world figure when for the first time at Chicago, 11th Sept. 1893, the best men and women of the modern world heard and immediately accepted wholeheartedly, the ancient Indian wisdom that God is One, though religions may call it by different names. And, not just belief in this or that religion, but a virtuous life of righteous conduct, serving God in man, supported by daily prayers, meditations, etc. is the universal way to attain ‘Perfection’.

Who is responsible to chastise erring Preachers? Local administration, Governance, Courts? Who is responsible to see that the preaching’s are universal and not narrow? That the preaching’s of all religions must inspire us to imbibe and act in accordance with the five-universal human values, e.g . love, peace, ahimsa, righteous conduct, truth. That we may adjust, adapt, accommodate with the idea of God in other religions. After all God is merciful and can understand the devotion and love of a true worshiper; and, being infinite there could be unity in diversity.

Responsibility of Governance and Schooling: Fortunately, imbibing these five-values has now been made and integral part of NEP20. And, earlier the government of India, MHRD, had identified various reputable NGO’s belonging to various Religions, who were involved in spreading the importance of imbibing universal values, and Books have been produced by various Religions on the importance of imbibing these universal values. Besides teaching of subjects, it is up to the academia, parents and reputed NGO’s to together inspire all citizens to get engaged in daily, regular, essential self-development practices.
