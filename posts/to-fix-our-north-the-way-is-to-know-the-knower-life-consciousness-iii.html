India to Bharat · Essay #108

To fix our ‘North’, the way is to know the ‘knower’, life, consciousness (III)

God, Creator, as Consciousness: Our body is just an inert matter. Somehow it becomes a living being and makes us conscious! Shouldn’t the best of us try to investigate what the entity called ‘life’, ‘consciousness’ is all about? The best of our ancients had focused on investigating and exploring how can an inert body become a living being aka jivatman? They ‘saw’ the obvious reality that it is life, consciousness, spirit, the ‘Kingdom of God’ within each of us that transforms the matter-body into a conscious being. So, they explored this consciousness or Atman, and its source, which gave life to the inert body, and found Man’s North.

Their findings and analysis is being discussed here to fix our highest, common goal, through a common-sense principle of “cause & effect”, that the ‘effect is one with’ and ‘not separate from the cause’. However, ‘cause is independent and separate from effect’. So, their conclusion is that the creation (living beings and nature) is one with Creator, God, who eternally exists everywhere and is the one singular consciousness, the subject, witness and knower of the infinite objects of creation; and only by experiencing oneness with creation can we be perpetually blissful.

So, our North must be to strive to grow in feelings of love, care, concern for more and more till we once again become one with the whole creation and the Creator, from whom we started!

Somehow, as modern intelligent educated people we’ve failed to study the “Knower, Observer, Thinker”, aka God, the one Subject, which knows and is itself the infinite Object in creation! Thus, we’re in a state of perpetual purposelessness, divided into ‘my vs your’, in a constant ‘fearful’ state of conflict, fighting with each other and ourselves and are unaware of purpose of living!

Oneness of ‘God-man-nature’ is established on the principle of non-separation and oneness of cause and effect

God is both the “Cause and material Cause” while man and nature are the “effect” just as Gold is the Cause and all ornaments are effect. The wide variety of ornaments, though appearing in various names and forms, all are only ‘indicators’, pointing towards Gold! In fact Gold can only be recognised by a “name and form” – and all the while the reality is that ‘Gold alone exists’ as the only reality!

Similarly there is NO separation between man/nature/God; they/we’re all ONE integral whole; ‘God alone exists’, though appearing as various names and forms aka Creation for some inexplicable reaso!

If the material-cause is destroyed, the effect cannot exist

For example, ‘Gold is the cause and if gold is removed from the effect, ornaments, then the ornaments don’t exist!

Therefore, the 1st Principle is that EFFECT, is not separate from CAUSE, i.e., creation is not ‘separate’ from God; Creation is ‘one’ with God. However, even though the effect is destroyed, Cause continues to exist.

Therefore, the 2nd Principle is, CAUSE is independent/separate from EFFECT though effect is not separate from cause. i.e., God is independent of Creation, though One with it!

Creation is not separate from God but God is independent of Creation. And, our North is to strive to know, realize, experience God and thereby be one with the whole Creation, by being good, virtuous, just, loving, fair, and equal for all, just as is God! Instead of dividing us all, religions and education must together teach us to be committed to righteous, virtuous conduct; the guaranteed path to North.

Worldview of ‘oneness’, not ‘separateness’:

We’re all one; not just inter-connected, interdependent, and interrelated. This ‘oneness’ was recognized by Bharat’s so-called superstitious, ignorant, uneducated ancient civilization! Why has so-called modern educated scientific society failed to investigate the ‘knower’, consciousness that makes us a living being?

Why have we allowed self-centered politicians and religions to divide us? Why have we failed to evaluate people as good, better, higher, lower, based on their progress towards feeling of ‘oneness’ with more and more and making them our mentors, leaders, administrators?

Envisioning the future

People are in different stages of growth and development in their journey towards the highest Goal of life, viz. the direct ‘seeing’, ‘experiencing’ our oneness with God and with the whole creation, a state of perpetual Poorna-ananda, total-fulfilment-bliss, and equal love for the whole creation. This is our North which we attain when we can purify our mind through righteousness and become established in ‘oneness’ with the Atman, ‘Kingdom of God within us!

It is from this goal of Poorna-ananda that arise all religions and our ideas of ‘do good, be good’, virtues, ethics and all morality and righteousness, dharma and our respective swadharma. Every human being is on this journey towards North, i.e., directly experiencing our Oneness with each other and with the whole creation, i.e., with God, the Creator.

Education

During schooling and growing up, it is not only also possible, but desirable, to let every young person discuss, explore, reflect, fix our ‘North’ and choose a vocation and a self-development plan, best suited for the journey. We need mentors as parents, teachers and academicians to show us how to be righteous.

Purpose of life

Those who strive and reach nearer to this idea of ‘oneness’, are the ‘shrestha’ mentors; comparatively more likely to be fair, just, committed to dharma and fit to be teachers and role models of societies. Religion is not mere ‘belief’, ‘belonging’ and bragging’. And purpose of life is to regain our lost Kingdom of God thruough righteousness; not just for hierarchical promotion from “lower to higher caste/status” or “worker to ruler” or “poverty to wealthy”, etc.
