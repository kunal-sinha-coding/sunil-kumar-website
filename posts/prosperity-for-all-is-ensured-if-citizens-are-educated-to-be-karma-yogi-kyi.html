India to Bharat · Essay #096

Prosperity for all is ensured if citizens are educated to be Karma Yogi, KYi

If we reflect on the cosmos and its design and discuss and study the working plan of Creation from childhood and growing up, we will learn to appreciate that behind seemingly chaos and accidental happenings there is and must be an intelligence (symbolically may be called a deity) which maintains ‘order’, Rtm, a balance and harmony based on interconnectedness and mutual cooperation, in this Universe (signifying One, united entity – Song!).

And this Rtm is maintained because everything is designed to and does follow their respective inherent duty, Swadharma! Except of course we the wise human beings, who have been created in the ‘image of God’; and so, have the freedom to do our Swadharma or not do it – and so we suffer the consequences of not following the universal Virtues – principle of love, truth, peace, righteous commitment to our prescribed duties, etc.

Thus, just like this God’s Creation, our common Uni-verse, where every piece of inert-matter, all-natural forces, and all living-beings are always doing what they’re designed for every moment, so also every ‘Institution and Society’ must be nurtured to work in mutual harmony and cooperation.

However, since man has been given the freedom of choice and intellect to follow or not to follow its duties, it is only man who needs training of ‘stick and carrot’ so that using his intellect he can ensure that in and thru’ his daily working and living he ‘Regains (literal meaning and real purpose of Religions, Religare in Latin)’ and unites (literal meaning of Yoga) once again, with the Creation and Creator, that Singular, Infinite Consciousness of the nature of perpetual bliss, aka God, called by various names and worshipped in as many forms, etc.

Thus, both education and Religions must work together to teach man the needed practices to regain his lost state of perpetual bliss, as ‘happiness’, that we try to seek in our daily work and actions. So, man needs religions, education, training, parenting, spiritual mentors, Preachers to learn to build powers of mind to know and be committed to duties and to righteous, ethical, virtuous conduct. Only thru’ this training during the first 25 years of life, can we as citizens learn to walk-the-talk, and choose the long-term good and eschew the short-term pleasures.

And each individual human-being and every citizen of every nation must be trained and educated during growing up to remain committed to striving to imbibe the spirit of becoming a Karma Yogi, KYi, by learning and practicing to ‘work’ selflessly while performing their universal duties, Dharma, and respective duties, Swadharma, in vocation and life.

Instead of Religious fanaticism, self-centeredness, fighting and killing each other trying to prove their God and limited Scripture as the only keeper of Infinite, Omniscient Consciousness that Truth is, all Preachers of Religions must learn to work together with each other and with political leaders and academia so as to create Action Plans for Training of School Teachers, Students, Citizens, to inspire them to strive to be Karma Yogis committed to their respective duties and mutual co-operation and harmony, Rtm.

It is Dharma, Swadharma, Virtues, Order, Cooperation – Rtm, that sustains this universe. It is Rtm, that protects each of us from misery. It is Rtm that protects he who protects Dharma! It is Rtm that maintains the principle of ‘as you sow, so shall you reap’. We’re what we’re, good or bad, based on commitment or otherwise to our Dharma and Swadharma – I and I alone am responsible for whatever I am; I am the creator of my destiny.

All our long-term goals must be based on the idea that it needs Character and Character alone to become one in harmony, Rtm, with the whole creation and this is our universal North; the goal of Ananda, of perpetual bliss that passeth understanding . Unfortunately ignoring this holistic worldview of an Interconnected-Reality, modern society is brought up on the petty ideas of ‘my-vs-your’ and thus education, schooling and growing up focus only on the 3R’s and this and that subject and Degrees but totally ignore the idea and ideal of Education and Religions for Character-building!

We cannot build character thru’ mere marks, degrees, lectures, and textbooks! To change our worldview, our tendencies we’ve to get established in self-development processes and habits to awaken our viveka-buddhi and strength of mind to learn and practice knowing and choosing the long-term good and be committed to selflessly fulfilling the universal duties of love for all and for our respective duties.

And this is the teaching of all Religions and all Rishis, Prophets, Godmen, viz. learn and practice being virtuous and being committed to our respective duties and to love, care, concern for each other; we’re all God’s children. And Krishna as Mentor, elaborates this idea of KY to Arjuna, the Mentee:

# It is our obligatory duty to act, to work, perform karma, in the spirit of Yajna, i.e., prayerful offering in form of sacrifice of selfishness for universal well-being and all-round prosperity.

# This is the secret of action, of Karma Yoga, kaushalam of Yoga because of which Arjuna is asked to fulfil his duties and is told, ‘Prosper thru’ selfless performance of your respective duties, yajñas (sacrifices); and as a consequence, in return, the forces of nature will in the same selfless spirit bestow upon you all you wish to achieve.

# This spirit of Rtm, harmony, maintains this universe and we help maintain Rtm by our selfless actions to fulfil our respective duties which impels and propels the deities who seemingly govern the forces of nature, also to harmoniously do their duty…. Contd.
