India to Bharat · Essay #022

Why start with ‘love all’ in sports? ‘I am you’; ‘oneness’ is the reality behind all goodness

“I love you” because I AM YOU is an intuitively felt reality that we experience at some moments and Life itself is a training to feel Oneness with more and more as we grow from an individual towards oneness with wife, family, community, etc.! Politics & Religions now Divide; not Unite!

Academia must Teach “Unity in Diversity” to transform Belief-based Religions into “Work as Worship”, not mere My Vs Your God!

India that is Bharat, alone has the vision and worldview of ‘whole world as our family’! Of our Oneness as Life or something like it; of an unknown Changeless, a Unity, around which alone, changes can happen; a Conscious-changeless Unity, aka God, that keeps the ever-changing cosmos in Order, rhythm, Rtm!

The idea and the ideal of this unity come from the similarity in design of the “solar system, Macrocosm (God)” & “Living-being, Jiva-atman, Microcosm”.

It is obvious that Identified as Consciousness, Life, Self, we’re all one with each other! That’s why Love, not Hatred is a universal idea and ideal taught by all, even by religions!

While today we all identify with the ever-changing body-mind, the higher purpose of education, religions, sciences, vocation, work, must be to learn and practice imbibing the higher ideal that as Conscious-Self we’re really One; not different, not separate! We’ve to consciously go beyond the apparent ignorance that I am only this changing body-mind. And consciously imbibe ‘seeing’ one ‘Conscious-Existence’ (aka One My & Your God) in all and in everything and everywhere.

Only thru continuous striving for Righteousness and treating every Duty, and all Work as Worship can we struggle, practice and actually experience our Oneness as Conscious-Existence. Schooling and growing up is an opportunity to explore, reflect, study, discuss the merits and demerits of this unifying, loving, idea and ideal of our oneness.

“Believing” Vs “Actually experiencing”: Belief must result in direct experience! Modern society is led by Religions, Preachers, Academia focused on Knowledge as Believing! We must wonder, why is it that the ancient civilizational culture of India that is Bharat still stands today, in spite of physical & religious attacks and onslaught on us by invaders for thousands of centuries with their critique of all our ideas!

The Healthy Worldview of Reality on which from the beginning of the creation, stands the wisdom of India that is Bharat, is expressed in the following ideals:

i) Based on our own self-acquired ‘past memories’, mindset, Samskaras, people are different from very birth (that’s why they’re born in different families!).

ii) Minds of all people are continuously being formed (purified & refined or polluted) by their deeds and are mixtures of 3 Qualities of Sattva (calm), Rajas (activity), Tamas (passive), SRT, in varying proportions. Purpose of work and life is to gradually refine the Mind so that it manifests the power and bliss of Consciousness within and Re-gains its unity as One-Consciousness (meaning of Religion & Yoga).

iii) The Best Practice we need to follow is to re-create the ideal social systems where daily growing-up, learning, living and working itself, is a planned education and training to gradually grow from Tamas to Rajas to Sattva thru’ learning and imbibing the spirit of selflessness, righteousness, learning to treat ‘work as worship’ of ‘One Conscious-Existence’, aka God in man and in all Creation. This purifies and expands the mind as we consciously help and serve; and we become more compassionate, loving, caring and we unconsciously experience the “oneness, peace, happiness” already within!

iv) Our time-tested systems are based on planned growth from Tamas to Sattva and beyond, in contrast to modern ideas focused only on Money-Pleasures ignoring commitment to Righteousness.

v) Our systems are pithily expressed as: {Dharma. Artha. Kama. Moksha}; {Brahmacharya. Grihastha. Vanaprastha. Sannyasa}; ‘Brahmin-Kshatriya-Vaishya-Shudra’}

“Thinking. Believing. Inferring. Knowing. Perceiving” thru’ Sense-organs if Righteous and based on love and selflessness must lead to “Direct Realization & Experiencing” of our Oneness!

Religions and Academia must teach and build powers of mind so that we may single-mindedly, fervently, passionately, selflessly, strive thru’ duties, work as worship, righteousness, to know the truth of Truths behind the Creation.

Modern Believers, Preachers and followers of different Religions, remain narrow, petty-minded self-centered simply because they don’t act based on their Beliefs. Their intentions, thoughts, words, deeds don’t reflect the wisdom of the Prophets.

Children today Believe that Milk comes from shops and in bottles! A frog in the Well believes, nothing could be bigger than my Well, and idea of Ocean is a lie! Can infinite idea of universe or its infinite Creator, be ever confined to one idea or belief? Where’s the Unity in Diversity?

And in Democracy, hunger for power-artha-kama, get mixed with Belief and becomes responsible for killing, violence, terrorising and spread of Hatred for Others. We forget the ideal of love, equality, righteousness, peace, love thy neighbour as thyself, etc.

It is ‘direct Experience’ that Inspires, not mere ‘Believing’! The Prophets and Founders of all Religions and their holy words and symbols, still draw devotees because they are based on direct realization and experiencing, revelations. It is only on the basis of direct experience of Rishis, Saints, Sages that the Vedas, Upanishads, Ramayan, Mahabharat, are still in fashion today!

As we sincerely strive, spontaneously we will grow in love, care for more and more, and deeper, profounder understandings will burst forth from within – and we will actually Experience the truth of this creation and Creator – the same reality that is experienced by all who believe and then experience!
