India to Bharat · Essay #133

Discussions to understand God – our goal and purpose of life

Modern problem: ‘If you don’t know where you are going, any road will get you there’! We ’re in a state of perpetual purposelessness, busy dissecting and exploring the world of matter, blissfully unaware of the idea of God, the Consciousness that illumines our mind and intellect, the very Cause of this universe and the ultimate goal and purpose of our lives! Somehow, modern IQ and scientifically oriented world has totally ignored logical discussions on these basic issues. Fortunately, the scientists of Vedanta have discovered these truths of Truth and have documented their findings and present their research for all mankind to study.

Questions: Can this highly complex, mathematically and intelligently designed universe come out of nothing, accidentally as a big bang? Shouldn’t the first men be highly intelligent to survive, create means of survival, various languages, etc.? Could our ancients be monkeys and savages? How could they create the Vedas, languages? Shouldn’t there be a Creator, idea and design of creation, and spiritual guidance for the very first men? The first men, say the Vedas, have to be the seers and sages, the Rishis who give spiritual guidance, various languages, codes of conduct and kick start what we call the most ancient Vedic civilization for all mankind. It is the Vedas that are the very first discoveries and discussions on That-infinite-Consciousness, Brahman, God. All scriptures, Prophets, Godmen, the Vedas, are the property, and treasure trove of spiritual wisdom for all mankind. It is these revealed god-words that clarify: who am I, why am I here, where am I going, how to get there!

2. Unfortunately, selfish, petty-minded religious-preachers have failed to appreciate the essence of Consciousness and so have failed to work together to bring this essential wisdom for the benefit of all mankind. Science has now become the intellectual search for Truth but without spiritual consciousness and spiritual practices, science remains in a state of perpetual purposelessness lost in the maze of matter, meandering in the marvels of technology for entertainment and comfort!

3. Understanding the idea of Upadhi to know anything: Upadhi is a very interesting and profound word; so, let’s analyze it logically and deeply! Upadhi is that by which we are enabled to see, understand, and grasp the nature of any object. Real Swaroopa, ‘unchanging inherent form’, of a person or anything or even God, is known only when we see it free from all qualities, qualifications, names and changing forms, i.e., upadhis, which help us differentiate and grasp it! When we indicate a person as male, female, engineer, doctor, short, dark, with a beard, etc. these are all upadhis to ‘know’ a person; just as a Passport is our international upadhi! However, it is to be noted that in the absence of upadhi also the person remains!

When we become used to seeing a person with upadhi, say beard or spectacles or body, we fail to recognize him without upadhi. Thus, without the upadhis we are not able to grasp and differentiate many things! In presence of upadhi we do get an idea of object but it may not be a clear understanding. We have to investigate further. For example, when Upadhi is closely sticking together or identified with object like clay is to the pot, or gold is in the bangles, we have to separate it mentally; we cannot remove clay from pot or gold from the jewelry!

Only he who knows the Swaroopa can really describe the inherent nature of a thing, others depend on upadhi alone! We deal with each other but do we really know each other! Only Shruti, Rishis, Prophets, realized souls, can know and ‘talk’ of the qualities, distinguishing features swaroopa of God, Brahman, which is formless and upadhiless! However, it is very profound to reflect and appreciate that they can but ‘tell’, talk of it, but cannot reveal the Swaroopa!

So, we suddenly realize that even ‘words’, ‘languages’ are only upadhis, trying to indicate the feelings, emotions, thoughts arising deep within us. That is why there’s communication gaps. ‘Language is not different from meaning’, but ‘meaning is different from the language!’ Thus, words as upadhis can only point towards our feelings or the Swaroop of a thing; but the actual realization of the Swaroopa has to be done by each individual. When it is difficult to know the Swaroopa of each other, imagine how can we know the Swaroopa of God, which is infinite!

4. A doubt: When upadhi cannot be separated from God or even each other, then is it really possible at all to get an idea of the swaroopa of each other or God? Yes. We point to a cow; and even though their upadhis like colour, form, size, etc. differ, the child has the in-built ability to understand their cow-ness, and recognize all types of cows; mentally disregarding their different upadhis. We’ve the ability to mentally filter out the upadhis of words and grasp the meaning. Multiplication is only an upadhi to understand the ‘result’ of multiplication! We are doing this process of freeing the upadhis from objects, all the time when dealing with external world. But when it comes to freeing God, Brahman from upadhis, we find it very difficult!

5. All spiritual practices offered by various religions are attempts to inspire man to follow righteous conduct, Karma-Yoga, fulfill his Swadharma for good of society, pray, worship, etc. These purify the mind and only a pure mind can realize God, the omnipresent Consciousness. Education can help us discuss, recognize and understand that the goal of life is to realize that the whole world is our family, not divided into my God vs your God, etc.!
