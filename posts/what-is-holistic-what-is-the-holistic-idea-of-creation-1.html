India to Bharat · Essay #168

What is holistic & what is the holistic idea of creation? -1

Mystery of the Whole : Modern society is running its affairs without any idea of the holistic plan and purpose of creation and is therefore, said to be in a state of ‘perpetual purposelessness’ ! Understanding the idea of the whole will inspire us to run our lives and affairs in harmony with Creation and will lead to Holistic Education, Management, Governance.

Knowledge of the ‘whole’ picture is an absolute must for solving a Jigsaw puzzle. Similarly, we need to know the ‘whole’ picture, plan and purpose of creation. Without knowledge of the whole, we think of ourselves only as individuals, unconnected with others of creation, and so we become ‘asur’, materialistic, selfish. Only if I start ‘wondering’ will I start to understand and struggle to work and live in ‘sur’, in harmony with Creation.

Existence itself is Whole: Holistic means whole, complete, total, fulfilled, poorna . Can something that keeps changing be considered whole? Or does it have to be constant and unchanging to be whole? We can reflect upon and try to imagine the idea of whole creation as ‘that which exists’; ‘existence per se’. That which exists can never go out of existence , though it may change its form. This is a Scientific principle. Water exists but disappears, becomes cloud and reappears but now existing as rain water. Seeds exist; disappear. Now plant exists; dies; and so on. The whole-exists eternally, but in infinite different names and forms. Thus, if I exist now, I am e xistence per se; I can never go out of existence ! I am the whole creation; though being unaware of my wholeness! In fact, even as a separate individual a little reflection will reveal that I just can’t come into being and survive without existence of creation, which provides everything I need! Existence is the very cause of my being!

If I exist , and I do; and, I need no further proof of it, because I am conscious of my existence! If I exist then so do my parents and all my forefathers, including the very first beings, up to Adam, God, by whatever name or form we may conceive of beginning of creation and God.

So, whole is ‘That’ which exists eternally and is ever existing in the past, present, future; whole is that which remains constant and changeless in the past, present, future. There can be no time when the whole was not present, and there will be no time in the future when the whole will not be in existence. Whole is one integral whole which cannot be divided into parts, it cannot be destructed, and being whole it has to be nameless, properties less, formless and cannot be of any particular form or quality. Anything that changes its form signifies death; a seed sprouts, becomes a plant, has flowers, and in time decays and disappears from whence it came. But the whole remains whole.

Universe : All this creation is one whole (uni+verse; one-song; Om) existence; though infinite activities appear to be taking place! Science has also now realized that matter though appearing whole, within it there is constant motion! That from which existence appears to come is whole. This creation is whole and when the whole creation disappears, it emerges back into the whole. And the whole ever remains the whole. This is the holistic mystery of us, of Creation, of God. In fact, it is not really a new ‘creation’ but is just a projection which manifests for a time.

The Problem: All ‘existences’ such as changing forces of nature, sun, earth, seasons, plants, living beings, mother-nature, all these are following their respective duties, swadharma . Only human beings have the freedom of choice to follow or ignore their duties and the laws of nature. We gradually over time learn that we suffer pain and miseries when we misuse our freedom and ignore our duties and don’t follow righteous conduct. So, we humans need discipline, punishment and rewards; we need education, religions, management, governance, churches and temples, societies, to teach us to be in ‘sur’ , in harmony, with the plan of whole- existence , the projected creation.

The Purpose: Human life is an opportunity and process of learning how we can avoid pain and miseries by building Viveka-buddhi thru’ prayers, etc. and using Viveka to be virtuous and to know and fulfill our swadharma. Thru’ these our mind gradually expands in feelings of love and oneness till we once again ‘regain’ and ‘experience reunion’ (i.e. goal of religion), our oneness with the whole!

Some basic Questions? Since it is questions that open the door to knowledge let our systems of education and training inspire us to seek answers to some basic questions relating to the plan of Creation, e.g. Who are we? From where have we come? Why? For what purpose? Where are we going? Is there something more than living, working, dying? Is there a God? And so on, and we will see how our seers have answered them and try to see, with spirit of enquiry, reflection and Sraddha, whether what they say stands to reason.
