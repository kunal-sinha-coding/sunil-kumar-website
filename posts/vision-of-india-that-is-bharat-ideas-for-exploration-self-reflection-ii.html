India to Bharat · Essay #118

Vision of India that is Bharat – Ideas for exploration & self-reflection II

1. Reflection: Have self-obsessed modern Religions and corrupt Politicians compromised the value of “nation-first” to such an extent that one has to doubt if the present lot of so-called “3R’s-educated- leaders” will be able to draft “even one page of the Constitution” by setting aside their differences?

# The Bharatiya ideal is the ancient Worldview of “oneness”; of the ‘whole world as our family’ towards which all education, religions and governance must inspire us. Unfortunately, the modern worldview is being allowed to veer towards the narrow and petty ideas of ‘your-religion-your-party’ versus ‘my-religion-my-party’?

# As long as there is selfishness in the heart, so long, love of God is impossible; it is nothing but shop-keeping – Most of us seek something in return. We don’t need religions of fear and of temptations, of the fear of hell and temptation of enjoyment in heaven; but a religion of love; love for all equally.

# Education must imbibe in us a love that wants nothing, that does not care for heavens, does not care for anything in the world or the world to come – The ideal of love for love’s sake, work for work’s sake, duty for duty’s sake?

# We must imbibe the universal prayer, Lord, grant me this, that I may have love for Thee, and that for love’s sake

# We all believe in a formless, Impersonal God, immanent in the Universe, of which everything is a manifestation.

# However, our mind hankers for something concrete, symbols, images, even something we can grasp, talk to!

# Idea of a Personal God is therefore of great help in focusing our thoughts towards an ideal of highest formless idea and ideal of Perfection!

# We do get confused when we believe in all-merciful, all-powerful Personal God and then see so much injustice, pain; but we must not forget that the world runs on the basis of cause-effect; as we sow, so we reap!

2. Religions and sects are not the work of hypocrites and wicked people who invented all these to get a little money, as some of our modern men want to think. They are the outcome of the necessity of the human soul.

# All Religious pathways are necessities for different classes of human minds; and, will remain, despite any criticism.

# We tend to forget the teaching of love, compassion, forgiveness of the Founders of our religions but foolishly start believing selfish petty-minded preachers, who divide instead of uniting us!

# In this vision of love for all, we harmonize all philosophies, religions, all education, work and life, without any text-torturing!

# As an atheist, simply doing all work, selflessly, for good of all, can itself lead the way to our highest goal.

# Similarly, all rituals, ways of worship, sincerely done, lead to love on the condition that the heart of the worshipper must be purified and sincere, thru ethical conduct.

Our Spiritual Vision “rising up and up, step after step, from the gross to the fine, from the fine to the finer, until it reaches the Absolute, one with the whole creation, the Goal.

# The Leaders of Religions and in Governance must be those who are imbued with tremendous Rajas, Power, Energy and at the same time be living in the midst of the most wonderful renunciation – to produce such Citizens is our Ideal of Schooling and Governance

3. Myths or Facts? Are Fake news and disinformation a new Phenomenon? Is ‘Belief’ without validation ‘fake’ news?

# History reveals that propaganda and disinformation are nothing new. With good or bad intentions, myths, beliefs, half-truths, have always been with us; and, as long as a group of people believe in a same ‘fictional’ ‘his-story’ we get along well, at least within our ‘fake-belief’ groups. We all live in a bubble of superstitions, ‘beliefs and a ‘worldview’ that we acquire or are made to acquire, good or bad.

# Two thousand years ago a group of people started believing in a new ‘god and way of worship’ and went around spreading their belief by fair or foul means and “killing” those who refused, physically or emotionally to believe in their ‘angels, devils, paradise, hell, witches’ et al!

# Again, after a thousand years, another group of people go around spreading their belief by fair or foul means and “killing” those who refuse!

# Perhaps 70% of the world have now been ‘forced’ to choose one or the other!

# All believers lock themselves into a mythological or ideological bubble, constantly fighting each other’s ‘beliefs’ but never directly ‘seeing’ the factual veracity of their beliefs and never daring even to question it.

# Matter and material wealth is the new ‘god’ of science and modern schooling is their ‘way of worship’; and science now rejects the “beliefs” of 70% of people!

4. Reflection: How can I imbibe the worldview that my goal and purpose is to be committed to my swadharma, my respective duties, and my respective daily prayers, meditations, worship, etc. so that I continuously grow towards feelings of love, care, for more and more… in and thru’ my daily life.
