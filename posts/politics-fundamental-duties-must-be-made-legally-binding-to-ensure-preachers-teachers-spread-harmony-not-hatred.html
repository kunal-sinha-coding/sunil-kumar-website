India to Bharat · Essay #019

Politics & religions divide us; fundamental duties must be made legally binding to ensure preachers & teachers spread harmony not hatred

1) Harmony & peace: Only thru’ Governance, Academia, professional institutes, etc. can we ensure that each and every citizen learns and strives to imbibe the spirit of ‘Constitutional Fundamental Duties’; of ‘Karma-Yoga’, KY (as all public servants are now expected to follow); and ‘G20 Nations Vision of One World. One Family. One Future’.

2) Some of our constitutional fundamental duties are: “To promote harmony and the spirit of common brotherhood amongst all the people of India transcending religious, linguistic, and regional or sectional diversities; to renounce practices derogatory to the dignity of women”, “To value and preserve the rich heritage of our composite culture”.

3) Are we teaching and evaluating based on our commitment to FD’s, KY, Oneness ? Curriculum in schools have to develop key qualities like regularity, punctuality, cleanliness, self-control, industriousness, sense of duty, desire to serve, responsibility, enterprise, creativity, etc.

4) Although not the only source, religion is a major source of value generation. What is required is education about religion, their basics, values inherent in them, and comparative study of philosophy of all religions – this is a Supreme Court judgment.

UNESCO also pleads for spiritual convergence and to promote dialogue between faiths where religious conflicts have become the order of the day!

5) The problem: We allow preachers to preach to young minds the narrow exclusive and dogmatic ideas, based on one Master, God, Prophet and one scripture; some misguided fanatics often preach even active hostility towards the so called non-believers… Obviously, the young minds will become narrow, if they are taught that all other views are wrong and we claim that our scripture alone is true and our God, religion as the only true way.

6) In modern scientific world we accept unity in diversity and, in an “Intelligent” Space-Time-Causality” Universe that is always in Rtm, Order thru’ the Intelligence governed by principle of Cause-effect, which applies to all beings and forces of nature, e.g. action and reaction are equal and opposite!

7) The problem is when we believe all men are born equal when we know it isn’t factually correct ; or our belief that man has only one life in this world and it is purely by chance or God’s arbitrary will that one is born able or disabled, rich or poor and one will be awarded eternal hell or heaven according to his deeds.

How can in a diverse world there can be only one way to attain Oneness with all. Perfection, Salvation, heaven etc. also cannot be exclusive and dependent largely on one’s being a ‘believer’ in the dogmas and creeds of a ‘religion’. What about universal values, virtuous and spiritual living! Can pure, noble, morality, etc. be tied up with just a formal religious belief! Can immoral and cruel behaviour for non-believers or conversions by hook or crook be justified!

8) Government’s human resource development ministry, academia & preachers must sit together and work out how the spirit of Harmony and Brotherhood of our Fundamental Duties can be introduced to train and evaluate citizens for Leadership positions whatever may be their areas of work.

Healthy holistic worldview: All mankind should try and can realize their real nature, their infinitude, by undertaking and harmonizing the four aspects and essence of all religions, i.e. Universal Disciplines or Self-development Practices, Praxis for controlling our inner nature, and realizing our innate divinity and Oneness with all as Life:

i) Work itself as Religion: Fulfillment of FD’s, all duties and work done as our highest worship.

ii) Devotion as Religion: Prayers and worship according to our respective religion.

iii) Meditation and Spiritual Practices as per our Religion: Preferably under guidance of a spiritual adept who has himself realized the goal of his religion.

iv) Self-study/Introspection as Religion: Not mere belief, but an Intellectual Conviction thru’ direct Study of inspiring books which discuss about various ideas of God, Universe, and our real nature as Body-mind-intellect-memories (inert matter) which become Living-beings only when Enlivened and made Conscious by LIFE or something like it, e.g. eternal, changeless, blissful Consciousness aka God!

If so, could our goal or salvation mean or imply, ‘to practice being a Witness’ and just being aware of thoughts, feelings, emotions flowing in our mind (I am the Subject; I am not the thoughts!). If I am just the Witness, and I can practice and learn to separate, I realize that I can see the separateness between I and my thoughts! Am I then the Subject, Knower, Observer – not the object to be perceived!

Can I then practice being free from all pairs of opposites, from dukha even sukha and with a pure mind be in a state of perpetual bliss – joy or happiness, totally independent of all externals, and not dependent on any condition whatsoever! Of course, difficult; but an Ideal.

If from young age we can all learn to discuss and reflect thus, though following our respective religions, surely some of us will strive and learn to be a witness! Let the majority just grow believing some dogmas or a God in heaven, faith or belief … instead why not some have the opportunity in this AI, information explosion era, to ask, enquire, explore, debate and try to know what Life itself, is all about!

Summary: Of course, we must hold on to the essence of our own religious beliefs and learn to be committed to our work and duties while striving to love and respect all people equally, being open to other beliefs and also have an opportunity to know the Unity that Life is!
