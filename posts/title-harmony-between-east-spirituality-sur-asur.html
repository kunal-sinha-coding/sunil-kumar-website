India to Bharat · Essay #121

Title: Harmony between ‘East & West’; ‘Spirituality & Materialism’; ‘Sur & Asur’

Vivekananda’s Message: Let’s check a little the constant looking back to the past. We want that energy, that love of independence, that spirit of self-reliance, that immovable fortitude, that dexterity in action, that bond of unity of purpose, that thirst for improvement, that expansive vision infinitely projected forward, and we want that intense spirit of activity, Rajas-tempered with Sattva, which will flow through our every vein, from head to foot.

Now the time has come when our worldview of ‘oneness’, of ‘the whole world as our family’ should be carried into practice, not only in India but everywhere.

Modern science is removing superstitious and crude religious beliefs; though they’re still better than making greed and money our new gods. The old pioneering spirit of the west is vanishing, giving way to the worship of gold, money, over-ambition. No nation, however strong, can stand on such foundations, and the history of the world tells us that those that had such foundations are dead and gone.

In the first place, we have to stop the incoming of such a wave of greed and money in India. The materialism of the West must be conquered by Spirituality. Therefore, it is our duty to listen, understand, and spread this worldview of ‘oneness’ to everyone, so that all ideas of my ‘belief, my religion’ vs your ‘belief, your religion’, fanaticism, terrorism, money and violent technological gadgets of modern science are overcome by spiritual growth towards love, care, concern for more and more till we feel ‘one’ with the whole Creation.

And so, this idea of ‘oneness’ will have to go from India; it has already come out from the clutches of scholarly debates. It is now agreeable to the men of wisdom of all religions, races, nations; but it will have to grow and increase and save the modern global civilizations.

The idea of God and the Devil, has a good deal of poetry in it. The difference between God and the Devil is in nothing except in ‘unselfishness’ and ‘selfishness’. The devil knows as much as God, is as powerful as God, only he has no love for all equally; and that makes him a Devil, a fanatic, a terrorist.

Apply the same idea to the modern world – excess of knowledge and power, without spiritual maturity, without holiness, makes human beings devils. Tremendous power is being acquired by the manufacture of machines and other appliances, and privilege is claimed today as it has never been claimed in the history of the world.

That is why it is now the responsibility of academia of all religions to come together on one platform to study, discuss, appreciate, spread, the ideal of our ‘oneness’, our inter-connectedness.

Materialism and all its miseries can never be conquered by materialism. Armies, when they attempt to conquer armies, only multiply and make brutes of humanity.

Spirituality must conquer the modern global civilization. The good of all religions, of all nations are waiting for the worldview, for the ideal that unites us in love; the world is eager for it. Where is the supply to come from? Where are the people who are ready to go out to every country of the world with the message of our oneness with the whole Creation? Where are the people who are ready to sacrifice everything, so that this message shall reach to every corner of the world? Such heroic souls are wanted to help the spread of truth, such heroic workers are wanted to go abroad and help to disseminate the great truths of Vedanta. The world wants it. We have drunk deep of the cup of pleasure and found it wanting.

Now is the time to work so that the universal spiritual ideals of all religions may become a part of mainstream schooling. Only this will prevent the self-centred politicians, and petty, narrow-minded preachers from spreading hatred for the ‘other’. The many details that create differences in rituals, manners and customs and social well-being, are not the integral part of any religion.

We need Harmony between East and West; between ‘my and your’ Religion. We can all ‘Teach and Learn’ from ‘Each Other’. Academia from different religions must take responsibility to create a Common Minimum Program for mainstream systems. We should not allow narrow preachers insisting upon and fighting over the differences in the non-essentials.

The essentials of all religions is obvious. Ethical, righteous conduct, care and concern for all equally and commitment to our respective duties and working selflessly for good of all are the essentials. Let Academia and Preachers teach and inspire people to being ethical.
