India to Bharat · Essay #110

From ‘perpetual purposelessness’ to ‘fixing our common North’ (To be contd.)

Need to unite towards best: Modern society is being divided and polarized by ideas of democracy and religions; and, we’ve no idea and clarity to ‘unite’ societies and define and differentiate between good-bad, right-wrong, higher-lower. We continue to be in a state of purposelessness. Let’s try to discuss, explore and fix a higher, better, common Goal based on common sense and wisdom of “oneness”, towards which we all can learn to strive.

Today academia and education focus only on IQ, 3R’s, marks, degrees, not being good. Science focuses only on knowing nature built of ‘inert-matter’ but ignores study of ‘being good’ or ‘Life, Consciousness’ that makes us ‘Living-beings’, different from ‘matter’. Religions are focused only on ‘Belief and Belonging’ to ‘My vs Your God/religion’, Rituals/Worship, etc.

Modern society, for various obvious reasons, has thrown out Religions from the mainstream systems of education. However, the need and the ideal would be if all can sit together and work out our common, universal highest and best Goal, ‘North’, and show us how to inspire and make ‘good people of character and wisdom’, who will be Mentors of society and can then train us to develop powers of mind to walk towards the North.

Schooling and parenting, besides 3R’s, must also focus on inspiring people to be ‘good’ and help them build powers of mind, discrimination, will, self-control, etc. to remain committed to being good by being committed to Righteous, ethical actions and respective duties for good of society as a whole, and together walk towards the good, higher, better, ‘Best’ in and thru’ our daily work and living!

Our hypothesis is that governance, academia, science, religions must also study various available views and worldviews and let schooling and parenting devote time to explore, reflect and arrive at some common ideas on God and purpose and goal of our daily work and life, too! Only then can we draw action plans to inspire us all, to together build the ability to strive to be good, better, towards the best.

From ‘my-vs-your to Us’, a healthy Worldview of ‘Oneness’: Let’s together try to form an idea of our North and being good and, taking cue from religions, we will do this by trying to understand and appreciate the relationship between Man and Nature and an entity called Life or God, which is an ideal of “Perfection” towards which religions inspire us to go.

Of course the problem is that God is totally invisible and unexplored; even by our so-called educated and scientific era. Many believe and many don’t believe in God; while those who believe in God have no clarity or agreement over who exactly God is and, the believers of this or that religions themselves keep fighting, killing, spreading hatred and terrorism, in God’s name! Education and Science reject religions because religions ask us to have faith and ‘belong’ but don’t allow questioning. However all religions, education, society ask us to be good, not bad!

What, if any, is the Relationship between ‘God and Creation’? Let’s reflect on a Fundamental Principle and common sense reality, that the ‘Effect is One with’ and ‘not separate from the Cause’. However, Cause is independent and separate from Effect.

What will be the relationship between God and Creation, based on this principal of “Cause and Effect”? (To be Contd.)
