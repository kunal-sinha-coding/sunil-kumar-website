India to Bharat · Essay #109

Relationship between ‘God & Us’ based on the Principle of ‘Cause & Effect’: Fixing our ‘North’ II

1. We’re trying to fix our “North” based on a common sense Principle that the ‘Effect is One with’ and ‘not separate from the Cause’. However, ‘Cause is independent and separate from Effect’.

2. Worldview of all Religions and Science: All religions believe and see God as Omnipotent, Omnipresent, and Omniscient. And consider God, as a super Intelligence that has created the world of man, living-beings, and nature. Science is now similarly calling this an “Intelligent Universe”!

2.1 A Scientific principle is, “that which exists can never go out of existence; and that which doesn’t exist can never come into existence”. So, if ‘man and nature’, this Creation exists, then man too must be eternal, maybe in different forms, i.e. Spirit/Life/God; indirectly accepting an ‘intelligence’.

2.2 Science and we all also accept that no inert object/matter can move on its own; without outside force/intelligence and yet we the ‘Living-beings’, (Jiva+Atman), whose bodies are just inert matter, ‘move about on their own! So, the conclusion must be that the inert-body is made conscious by an outside force, ‘intelligence’ aka ‘Consciousness’, ‘Life’, ‘Atman’, i.e., God.

3. Since God as the Creator is the Cause, and we’re the Effect, then we’re all one with each other and we’re nothing but God itself appearing in various names and forms! And, that’s why we’ve the Principle of ‘love thy neighbor as thyself’; ‘whole Creation is our family’; and all exhortations to be loving, kind, just, merciful, virtuous, etc. just as God is!

3.1 Views on purpose of Life, our common “North”:

# ‘Seek you first the Kingdom of God within, and his Righteousness and everything else will be added unto you’; ‘Be you Perfect as Father’.

# Thru ‘Dharma’, ‘Earn’ & ‘Enjoy’, to achieve Freedom/Mukti/North’ where Dharma represents Universal Values of Truth, Love, Peace, Non-violence, Righteousness and includes Swadharma which is Commitment to our respective Duties

# ‘Each soul is potentially divine’; ‘The goal (North) is to manifest the Perfection already within’; ‘Do this thru’ ‘Work, Knowledge, Mind-management, Devotion’; etc.

4. Cause-Effect Principle: If there is some link between God and Creation, then what were the ‘tools’ and ‘Material’ used by God to create this world? God itself has to be the tools as well as the materials used to create, since there was nothing else besides God!

A familiar question arises, where is God now? In temples, mosques, churches only or everywhere or ‘Up-there’? And, a logical and reasonable understanding must be that since God itself is the material of creation, God must be That Existence, that is always present eternally everywhere and in everything and in every-being; not just here but not there! God itself must be basic elements of creation, Space; Air; Fire; Water; Earth + Time; Law of Cause-effect!

There is a difference of course between nature which is just inert-matter that just ‘exists’ and Living-beings that ‘exist’ and are also ‘conscious’, and always seek ‘bliss’, even though ‘Bodies’ are just matter like all of nature is! And among all beings, only human-beings have the ‘intelligence’ and that is why it is said ‘man was created in the image of God’.

5. Thus God can be said to be that Existence in nature and that Existence, Consciousness, Bliss in Living-beings which is ‘one’ singular Consciousness, the only eternal, changeless ‘Subject’, that never changes, is all-pervasive, is always present eternally everywhere and in everything and in every-being; not just here but also there at the same moment!

6. The instructions manual for Creation & living: The various scriptures, among which the ideas of Vedanta are the 1st and most ancient of all, could be the basis on which God planned, designed, created this Creation; and ensured that the very first human beings must not be ‘savages’ but must know our North and the ethical Path to it! Of course, man looking for pleasures often forgets the path of Righteousness and so Prophets, Son of God, Sages keep coming to once again remind us of our North and the ethical path to it; and become Founders of various religions!

7. Vedanta, i.e., essence of Vedas, tries to establish thru’ questioning, open inquiry and logic, reasoning, discussions, etc. and presents the idea of Oneness between Man, Nature and God.

8. To stop the increasing ‘greed’ as well as divisions, communal disharmony, fanaticism, terrorism, etc. by religions and democracies, who have miserably failed to fix our common North and Universal values of Righteousness, we’ve to plan strategies so that the men of wisdom from Academia and different religions can sit together and create a picture and envision our North and the Path to it.

Modern society must sincerely re-establish the Worldview of ‘Oneness’, of harmony of all religions and educate us so that we can all be inspired to gradually ‘know’ and ‘walk towards North’.
