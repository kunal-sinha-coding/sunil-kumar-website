India to Bharat · Essay #115

‘Know Thy Self’ is the essential science & philosophy of all religions – not ‘my versus your’ religion

The essence of all Religions is , as was stated in ancient times, re-stated two-thousands years ago, and once again re-emphasized and scattered broadcast in the midst of the modern Western world, that ‘Each soul is potentially divine’ , i.e., we’ve infinite powers lying dormant within each of us. This reality that each soul is potentially divine, is not religion, it is both common-sense and a scientific fact! This knowledge must be made a part of mainstream schooling and growing up.

# This awareness of the divinity, the kingdom of God within, as inner-Self, Life, Consciousness, Spirit, Atman, Rooha , is obvious and needs no confirmation from any religions. We can easily infer that it is Life, as all-pervasive, omnipresent Consciousness, that alone makes us as Conscious living-beings, Jiva+atman.

Why be good, virtuous? Founders of all religions ask us to be virtuous because of the fact that they’re conscious of this obvious scientific truth that as Consciousness within us, we’re all one and interconnected.

# And therefore, shouldn’t the ‘best’, PhD’s, academicians and scientists, be curious and get interested in going “inwards” to explore, discover, ‘see’ what this Life within is all about? Why shouldn’t we inspire all students in mainstream education to seek this Consciousness within, which must be the source of potential? This essence of seeking within, is the foundation of our Bharatiya culture and USP!

# In fact, education literally means “To bring forth from within”, in Latin; and our idea of philosophy and religion is Darsan, i.e., ‘seeing’; not just belief’, not just ‘belonging’ to this or that religion, but to practice serving man, seeing the same kingdom of God, in everyone.

# Thus, ancient Greek philosophers had said ‘Know Thyself’. Striving to go within, to know the kingdom of God within, is education, religion, purpose of our vocations.

# This idea of God within every being and without, can dethrone all the Gods in heaven, in temples or elsewhere; it places on that Godly-throne the Self of man, the one omnipotent God which is higher than the sun and the moon, higher than the heavens, greater than this great universe itself! No books, no scriptures, no science can ever imagine the glory of the Self that appears as man, the most glorious God that ever was, the only God that ever existed, exists, or ever will exist, says Vivekananda; so, realize the highest goal of man by serving God in man as our highest worship; not as charity.

# ‘Recognizing divinity in oneself’, and ‘acknowledging the divinity in others’, is the ‘essence of all Religions’ and inspires us to be virtuous; righteous conduct is the passport that ultimately leads to ‘Direct Experience’.

Righteous Action, Karma yoga, is the highest and Universal Religion: This knowledge not only leads to harmony among religions but harmonizes all religious practices and focuses attention on the fruit of religions, viz. the need for commitment to ethical, righteous conduct and commitment to our respective duties, on being karma-yogi.

# This philosophical essence of all religions, to work for good of society, is the reason for the greatness of the Bharatiya culture, that India has now forgotten. It has to once-again become part of schooling, growing-up, and governance systems, which are all creating selfishness, corruption, fissures in society.

# Shorn of all religiosity and belief in this or that God or religion, a moral life alone guarantees the fruit of a religion. Only if we adhere to ethical, righteous conduct, follow spiritual practices and ideals of our respective religions will we gradually learn that instead of just worshipping God in churches and temple, it is good to practice seeing God in man and in each other and in the whole creation.

# All charitable endeavours are based on the principle that the highest worship is to ‘serve’ God in man. Unfortunately, we the educated and our leaders and preachers have forgotten this ‘essence’ which remains lost in the forest of scriptures, philosophical and intellectual reasoning, ideologies, mythologies, politics, etc.

# In absence of learning about our divine potential during schooling, instead of being a karma yogi and striving to work hard, unselfishly, and learning to work together for the good of society, we’ve become hopelessly disorganised mercenary mobs, immensely selfish, fighting each other!

# We must reflect upon and question our stupidity; and ask ourselves and our leaders, academia, preachers, as to why this Scientific truth has not so far been taught as an essential part of schooling! Shouldn’t it now be made an integral part of study of science during schooling and growing up?

It is our obligatory duty to study, discuss, reflect, and acquire an intellectual conviction in this divinity within. Knowing that I have the same God in me as you have in you gives us self-confidence, and this Vedantic awareness is what matches the idea of democracy; no undue privilege for any one, equal chances for all. I am responsible for what I am; it is up to me to strive to manifest more and more of my potential by striving to serve, to help…
