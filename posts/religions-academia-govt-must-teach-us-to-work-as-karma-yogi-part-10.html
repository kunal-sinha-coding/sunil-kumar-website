India to Bharat · Essay #145

Religions/academia/govt. must teach us to work as karma yogi (Part 10)

All citizens must be intellectually convinced and established in the idea, during schooling, that Work and Vocation done as Seva-bhava, in the spirit of worship, leads to the same highest Goal that all religions promise to take us to.

What is the Purpose of Work and Life? We may, and we do have various dreams, goals and engage in all kinds of work to attain them. We also discuss, judge, various issues as right/wrong; good/bad, etc., based on our respective goals. However, if from a young age we are given the opportunity to discuss, reflect, analyze, we shall realize that no decision, thought, action, can really be correct, in the long-term, unless we first fix the ultimate purpose and goal of working and living, say Perfection.

Perfection? We need to learn to constantly reflect: What are the various ideas of Perfection and; will my goals lead me towards highest logical, rational idea of Perfection? And, seeking the answers in an academic milieu is needed today so that, at least as a nation, without divisive political or religious ideology and belief, we can agree upon common principles to be learnt, imbibed, practiced during schooling and at work, to attain Perfection. If as students we get to study this issue from various angles, including our own culture we shall appreciate that it is good to be good! All religions tell us so, but immature preachers keep us busy in ideological beliefs and we miss the essence, “Be good and do good”; “Unselfish is more Paying”.

Fulfilment of Duties, Excellence in work done for good of society, care, concern for others, Fundamental Duties, etc. are part of our Constitution. Thus, government must direct all schools to instill these Attitudes. And, only those who imbibe this commitment, should be allowed to go for higher studies and recruited for political or leadership positions at work or in governance.

Only if, from a young age, we’re made to reflect , discuss, explore we shall come to the gradual realization that all our different goals are meaningless unless we’ve first explored and agreed upon the purpose and ultimate goal of our daily working and living. Only then we can discover some basic principles and values of righteous conduct that we as a nation and humanity should imbibe to attain fulfilment, Perfection.

We all work and so must be trained to be a karma-yogi: We may or may not believe in any God or Religion but we can all be educated and trained to have the necessary skills to practice being less and less selfish; and, more and more selfless! We need the skill to think and reflect, to be conscious every moment when we take decisions or a selfish thought comes, to ask: am I going towards the higher or lower? We need skills to Practice putting our whole mind on whatever we do, to strive for perfection with the conviction that perfection in daily work, life, whatever it may be, sports, acting, teaching, etc. is the path to highest Perfection.

We all can attain the goal of Perfection by practicing quality and excellence in work. When we see a man doing good work, helping others, it means he is growing towards Perfection. All religions, ethics tell us to practice unselfishness. As we practice, we expand to experience of oneness with more and more, towards Perfection.

The highest goal of mankind: All religions, ethics teach, do good, don’t harm. Why? Because we are all God’s children. The same Rooha, Spirit, Kingdom of God is within us all.

The truth is that we all, the whole Creation, emerge from One God, and so, regaining (Religion) and Unity (Yoga) with the Whole Creation, God, is the highest ideal of Perfection. We may call it Salvation, Mukti, Freedom, Heaven. And, can be attained by unselfishness; practicing care, concern for all. Every unselfish thought-action pushes us towards Perfection.

Equality or Inequality? Only One-God/Religion or many? Islam and Christianity believe all are equal, must follow One-religion. They try to convert everyone to their faith, by stick or carrot. This immature thinking by narrow-immature preachers has led to fanaticism, terrorism, and has made religions taboo in academia and democracy! Science has now emerged as the new religion in opposition to the Church.

Unity in diversity is the plan of creation: True equality has never been and never can be on earth. How can we all be equal here; follow one-god/religion? This impossible kind of equality produces religious fanatics and implies total death of creation.

Unity is only in and behind this diversity . What makes this world what it is? Lost balance, chaos, where we all struggle, compete, to regain lost Perfection. Absolute equality, that which means a perfect balance of all the struggling forces in all the planes, can never be in this world.

The Truth of truths is that we’re all equal not as body-mind complexes, but as Spirit, as Pure singular Consciousness aka God; also as children of God, as the same God resides in the heart of us all; so unity , equality or oneness with whole creation, with God, is to be realized by practice of seeing God in everyone, and serving man as God.

Selflessly working for good of society, of Creation, is Karma Yoga; it is the real essence of Religions. And, Karma yoga is the only way to actualize the political and democratic ideal, of liberty, equality, fraternity which can take us towards.
