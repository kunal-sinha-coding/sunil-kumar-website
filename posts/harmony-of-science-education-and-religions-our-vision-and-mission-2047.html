India to Bharat · Essay #099

Harmony of science, education and religions – Our vision and mission 2047?

India that is Bharat, alone has the wisdom, intellectual reasoning, and the age-old, time-tested civilizational culture, to spread globally this idea that Science, Education and Religions are all meant to guide and inspire us towards discovering the purpose of daily working and living and how to build the interest and powers of mind to strive towards becoming good, better, best.

It is this ideal of Harmony of Science-Academia-Religions that the world needs today. Harmony must triumph over and trump the idea that My-God-My-Religion alone is true; and this is now being globally recognized. Harmony, worldview of Oneness, focus on the Subjective, i.e., focus on inner spiritual awakening, and direct, individual realization of the highest truth, ‘Perfection’ aka God, is the USP of India that is Bharat, as indeed the essence of all religions, that we the educated and the world needs.

Science and Education are of no use if it cannot produce good, selfless people of character; and Religions and being religious, has no use and no meaning if religions focus on mere ‘Belief and Belonging’ while spreading violence, divisions, and ‘hatred’. All systems, teachers, leaders must focus on inspiring people to be committed to duties and to righteousness and love for all; and, be held accountable for failures.

The world needs academia and religion to work together to help and inspire us towards becoming good, better, best. Without imbibing and realization of inner-spiritual-realization can mere secular-education or divisive-religions and divisive-democracy help solve modern problems!

Therefore, the central message of India, which really is the essence of all religions, and that needs to be taught throughout schooling and growing up was and is, how to imbibe the spirit of tyaga and seva , selflessly working for good of all, aka Karma Yoga, to directly realize our highest Perfection. God realization is the goal of education and religions not mere ‘degrees and literacy’ or just ‘believing and belonging’.

Education, democracy, harmony of religions, are no doubt important, but only in the backdrop of this purpose and ideal of more and more unselfishness and less and less of selfishness. It is important to note this point. If we do not understand this vital point, we may misunderstand the idea and purpose of science, technology, education, and importance of harmony of religions. Forgetting this, today all systems of education, religions, politics, etc. instead of spreading selflessness and goodness are spreading clever ideas to grab money and power and divide people into ‘yours-mine’ camps.

All religions have two parts: the non-essential and the essential, the ceremonial and the spiritual, the external and the internal, the social and the individual. The spiritual portion is the essential and this must become a part of mainstream educational and growing up so that society may be inspired to become good, better, best.

Followers of every religion, led by petty selfishness and politics, make the mistake of mixing and confusing both these aspects into one. The problems generated by the societal and external aspect of religions get confused with the spiritual; and the result is that modern education and academia have rejected religions as superstitious and spreaders of ‘divisions and hate’, not of ‘unity and love for all’! Truth is infinite and so science and religions must work together; create an environment of seeking, exploring, questioning, reflection to discover the highest truth for themselves, instead of just belief and belonging.

Academia must help keep religion within its proper limits and give freedom to society to grow. Reformers and Scientists make the mistake of holding religion accountable for all the horrors of priestcraft, witchcraft, killings, and hatred spread in the garb of religions. And the result? Failure of society to make good people!

In India everyone made the mistake of holding caste to be a religious institution and tried to pull down religion and caste all together and failed; we failed to realize that despite all the ravings of the critiques, caste is simply a crystallized social institution for a stable society; just like modern organizations of the “Board, Directors, Managers, Skilled and Unskilled Workforce”. Elsewhere, globally, we’ve adopted modern 3R’s schooling and My-vs-Your God/Religions. And the result? As a modern society we’ve no method and no action plans to produce people of good character or a benchmark for character or a scheme to evaluate character and see that the best people are placed as teachers, mentors, leaders of society!

Education must embrace and include that ideal and self-development aspects of religions that inspires us to renounce selfishness and expand our heart to love all; this must be the focus of education and religions. Once a person is inspired to realize his true nature, he will appreciate that all religious are only attempts to educate our mind and intellect to strive to be righteous.

The idea of education and religions and harmony of religions divorced from the idea of making good people is dangerous, and it has resulted in all modern problems; and therefore, the need for a mission and focus on creating a new syllabus for establishing people in daily routine of self-development processes focused on producing people with character. And Governance to ensure that our teachers and leaders are those who are inspired to imbibe commitment to duties for good of society!
