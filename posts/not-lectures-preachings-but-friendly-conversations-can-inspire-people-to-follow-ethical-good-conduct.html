India to Bharat · Essay #015

Not lectures & preachings but friendly conversations can inspire people to follow ethical & good conduct

We’re only fit to be modern Academia, Governance and Leaders of societies, if we’re selfless and good people committed to our duties.

And, if we’re really selfless leaders, we must be mature enough to have discussed, explored and recognised the logical reality and fact that not just one, but various must be the ways and faiths to approach that infinite Omnipresent, omniscient, omnipotent Intelligence which we all revere as my vs your God!

And that love, care, concern for each other can be the only way God-wards; never ‘hatred’ for the ‘other’.

Thus, we all need to reflect – why have we all accepted religions and political parties that divide and spread hatred for the ‘others’, instead of cooperating with each other to ensure we’re all committed to selfless commitment to our duties.

We need to reflect on what is wrong with our worldviews, technologies, sciences, AI, etc that we, the educated and the teachers, pundits, religious preachers etc, have failed to inspire either people or leaders to be and do good!

Of course, as immature children, we must totally believe, depend upon and trust our parents. Later, as we grow into youth and adulthood, we discuss, question, argue, reflect, become adults and grow up with our own characteristics.

Similarly, let devotees believe in their respective religions, but it must be the responsibility of mainstream human resource development and professional training institutes, etc., to teach and give us an intellectual conviction that all Religions ultimately ask us to be and do good.

And only through’ righteousness, kartavya-palan, selfless work for the good of society, etc. can we build the ability to become leaders, Teachers and Preachers.

Our challenge as elders, governance & administration is to see how all educational teachings and religious preaching may be done openly and focused on ensuring that every citizen is provided an environment, space, time, inspiration for Praxis, i.e. daily self-study and self-reflection to awaken Vivek-buddhi to know and then build the ability to walk-the-talk towards the idea of good-better-best.

We all do realise how difficult it is to try and control the naturally self-centred and selfish desire-oriented tendencies of our mind!

Thus, more important than all teachings and preachings is to get established in the habit of daily self-development practices from a young age to first reduce the quantity of unwanted, negative and trivial thoughts flowing in our mind.

How? By building the habit of daily practices to plan, review, and direct our thoughts towards our immediate, short- and long-term goals.

We must also learn to be aware of the quality of thoughts flowing within our mind and then learn to practice improving the quality by pouring ‘good, better, best thoughts’ and then learn to direct and focus the thoughts towards the highest and best.

Only Praxis, daily practice and focus on the ‘higher, long-term selfless goals’, can help us to build the ability to control and gradually build powers of mind will, self-control, etc, to be able to learn to re-direct our way-wards Mind to focus on the higher long-term selfless goals of life.

In this AI and computer-era, modern Academia, schools, institutes, universities must take responsibility to learn, reflect, be intellectually convinced and teach us the basics that all mankind needs to openly discuss, explore, learn and know that Man is “Body-mind (that is matter) enlivened by “consciousness” (termed and understood as Life, Self, Atman, Rooha).

We all must grow up with the conviction that all beings are sacred and have the divine Self in them and all have to be guided God-wards, treating daily work itself as worship.

Academia and education must learn, teach, and inspire us to choose life skills and self-development processes that can accelerate our progress towards realization of our infinite potential.

Some believe it can take many lives, but some say we only have this life, and every moment we have the choice to opt for ‘heaven’ or ‘hell’ and the moment we choose, we book our respective ‘blissful’ or ‘miserable’ future – the Universal Intelligence ensures cause-effect principle prevails!

Ultimately, I and I alone am responsible to learn and acquire the knowledge and wisdom to walk the critical path to the ultimate realisation that, identified with Life, we’re all one, vast, infinite, interconnected, interrelated and interdependent all-pervasive blissful consciousness.
