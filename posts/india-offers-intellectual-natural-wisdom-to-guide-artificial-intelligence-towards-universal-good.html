India to Bharat · Essay #010

India offers intellectual & natural wisdom to guide artificial intelligence towards universal good

1) The Good & Bad of Artificial Intelligence, Ai: We’ve all realized by now, that all education, religions, and technologies, Ai included, can be used both for good and evil. However, we’ve yet to realize the truth that we do evil, hate, and wrong each other only because we’ve never ever been told that I am NOT this ever changing and decaying body, that comes and goes, and makes us selfish! Then what is my Unchanging Existence, the day of judgment, ‘Life’ or something like it? Can Ai not investigate and tell?

# Science, Technology & ‘Ai’, have united us all into an interconnected, interdependent One Global Family with a common Future – towards either ‘destruction and annihilation’ or ‘constructive realization of our ‘oneness’, as envisioned by all the good men, and the Founders of all religions and the galaxy of global Seers, Sages, Saints, Masters, Prophets, God-men, who all teach us, not hatred for each other but to love and live in peace with each other. Shouldn’t we wonder Why?

# However, modern Preachers of Religions have failed to become consistent and in harmony with the Reality of our Global ‘Oneness’! Preachers along with the educated Professionals and Political Animals in all walks-of-life remain narrow, petty minded and self-centered. And Preaching & Politicking together continue to Divide societies and spread hatred for the ‘others!

2) Our Challenge: First of all, we and Ai have yet to realize i) what our common Good is, and how to attain it and, ii) that mere knowing and gathering information and knowledge of all sciences and religions and making it available for Ai, means nothing!

Don’t we see that from infinite past we’ve available to us all the Vedic wisdom and for over 2000 years we’ve teachings, preachings, books of founders of all religious and yet till today we’ve no clue on how to be good and how to love our family let alone the neighbour as our own!

3) Need to Focus on our Inner nature – Mind: A Question arises, are our models of education, governance, preaching that focus mostly on the external, objective world really good? Aren’t we being totally stupid and foolish exploring the External universe of matter, merely for more knowledge as information, to satisfy our curiosity and for more comforts and enjoyments!

What is the purpose of all modern civilizational comforts and paraphernalia of science, technologies, Ai, preaching, schooling, education, etc., if we still remain divided, self-centered, hating, quarrelling in spite of all external knowledge and ability to fly to ‘moon’. And Why? Because of the habits, ideas, beliefs, memories as acquired by us and stored in our Mind!

4) Weren’t our ancients much wiser , when they asked: What is that by Knowing which everything is known? They reflected and realized that infinite is the external universe; we may keep exploring it thru’ good or evil, but it won’t ever be known, nor can it ever give us fulfilment, peace and bliss that we wish for!

So, the ancients all suggest that goodness is nothing but selflessness; and exhort us to strive to be more and more selfless and look Inwards to reflect, acquire new values and ideals of our Oneness, refine our Mind and past beliefs and then gradually realize thru’ goodness, that infinite knowledge and Bliss are already within our Self ! Of course, only a few will so strive and should be nurtured to be Mentors of society!

5) Look Inwards, they all proclaim: ‘Seek first the Kingdom of God within and everything else will be added unto you’; ‘Be you Perfect’; ‘Know Thyself’; ‘An-al-Haqq’; ‘Each soul is potentially divine’ and thus daily living and work in the spirit of worship and service purifies and expands our mind! Thus, refining our Mind to feel oneness with more and more, supported by our respective religious worship, prayers, etc. becomes the way to manifest the divinity within.

6) Knowledge is fruitful only as Wisdom: Mere knowing the external universe and how it all works is the search of modern societies and biology can tell us all about our bodies, brain, internal organs and mechanisms but today, Google, Wikipedia, AI can give us all such knowledge. Knowledge of the ‘External universe’ may satisfy the masses who focus on ‘Earning & Enjoying’ but it isn’t wisdom!

7) Knowledge as Wisdom is that by knowing and getting which, all that we wish to know and get, is attained. It is Wisdom that inspires a few good, wise men, to be and make mentors and imbibe and then walk-the-talk of selflessness, goodness for the long-term good of all towards a Utopia of Oneness with the whole creation, aka Perfection, Heaven, Freedom, etc. towards which we’re all headed whether we know, need, want or not!

We need to impress in our respective mind that Wisdom by knowing and imbibing which, we shall be intellectually convinced and willingly strive to be better, righteous, selfless, to get guaranteed fulfilment, joy that we seek!

8) Thanks to Ai we’ve now the possibilities that some wise men can ensure that Ai which has access to all religious scriptures, can scatter broadcast the Vedantic worldview of ‘One World. One Family. One Future’ and the way to attain the feelings of love, care, concern, Oneness with all, by striving to Imbibe the spirit of Karma Yoga, i.e. selfless commitment to Duties; Work itself as highest Worship…

We “fall in love” attached to finite-self & things; but we Rise in Love when we See & Worship God in all, thru’ Work.
