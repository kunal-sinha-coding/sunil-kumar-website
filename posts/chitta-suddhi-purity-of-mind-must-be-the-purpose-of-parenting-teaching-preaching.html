India to Bharat · Essay #082

‘Chitta-suddhi, purity of mind’, must be the purpose of parenting, teaching & preaching

1. Spirit of Oneness, Tyaga & Seva are the ideals of Bharat: Unfortunately, after attaining independence, our leaders have not only failed to display this spirit, but they’ve also failed to create systems of education, politics, and governance that inspires our citizens to strive towards Truth.

The greatness and sustainability of Bharat as a continuing civilizational ethos is our ideal of Sanatana Dharma, which simply refers to universal laws, principles, that are to be imbibed by all, irrespective of who we are, and the more we imbibe them the better are we suited to be a mentor, Shrestha, leaders of society.

We must be taught and learn to imbibe the spirit of whole creation as our family and commitment to righteousness and kartavya-palan; these are really the very purpose and the essence of education and all religions. Sanatana Dharma is not My-vs-Your Religion, but it is goodness, righteousness, etc. that each person has to imbibe during schooling and growing up!

2. Sanatana Dharma enjoins on us to devote 25 years of growing up and schooling to focus on creating citizens imbued with commitment to kartavya-palan and to love, care, concern for all equally, while selflessly working for long-term good of society as a whole; etc.

As a society we forget that whatever may be our God or Religion, the fruit of education and religions must be our commitment to imbibe universal human values of Truth, Peace, Non-violence, Righteous conduct, Love, etc. (Dharma) and be committed to all our respective duties (Swadharma)! Why? Because these lead to Chitta-suddhi, purity of mind, and only a pure mind can lead towards knowing and striving for good, better, best, aka Truth, God-wards.

Why should character building and being good not be a part of mainstream education, preaching, and promotion systems? History as well as the modern era is full of examples of polarization and ‘wanton killings, fanaticism, etc.’, in the name of My vs Your God/Religion. Why should preachers and teachers not be together held responsible and accountable to ensure that the young imbibe the universal values in our character during growing up?

It must be the responsibility of Governance to ensure that Academia and Preachers work together to ensure that educational and evaluation processes ensure that enlightened citizens are produced, evaluated, and promoted as leaders, mentors, teachers, of society.

3. Knowledge of subtlest “Truth” of God, Consciousness, Religions, cannot be imposed as a belief. In this era of science, reasoning, and Google, knowledge of “Truth” cannot remain a dogma, or a creed or a part of a religious belief system. And mere ‘knowing’ is useless unless our behaviour changes, as a consequence, towards Truth.

4. How do we Imbibe goodness? Pranipatena Pariprasnena sevaya is the Upanishadic instruction for walking-the-talk, i.e., “Repeated Questioning with humility; Seek to understand subtle Truth with spirit of humility through sincere prostrations, repeated questioning, and Service.

Highest, subtlest “Truth” of Consciousness of God is not a part of our experience. So constant Questioning (of the Guru, teacher, who has experienced “Truth”) and one’s own viveka and intellect is needed for understanding the wisdom, knowledge, which is not only beyond our experiences, but is beyond direct perception, being the subtlest, unperceivable Truth!

Questioning has to be a sincere seeking with humility and sraddha; it shouldn’t be casual or arrogant; and our mind should be sufficiently prepared, purified, chitta-suddhi, through righteousness, tyaga, seva, etc. to be able to sincerely enquire and seek not just to know, but to receive and imbibe the subtle truth.

In Mahabharat, Krishna scolds Duryodhan, ‘you haven’t learnt to serve elders and so, your mind is impure; learn to serve and build character otherwise you will remain arrogant’.

Modern idea of education and religions give us knowledge of 3R’s and scriptures and can be had from a teacher, preacher; and we can learn and memorize it too; but 3R’s cannot build character and neither can scriptures, unless we reflect and imbibe righteousness in our character!

Subtlest Truth of God as pure Consciousness are only inspiring when received from someone who has realized Truth, not thru’ mere preaching.

5. God as all pervasive Consciousness: Mathematically we can express the idea of infinite God and Creation in various ways, as:

i) God is that which is eternally existing and is changeless and unchanging!

ii) God is the Creator of world, i.e., Jagat, which literally means that which continuously changes.

iii) God is distinct from Jagat; but the Jagat is neither distinct from nor independent of God.

iv) World is God; God is all pervasive in the world; the world is in God.

v) God is the cause; world is the effect; World has no independent existence because it is only an effect.

vi) It is impossible to discern if Jagat is distinct from God or not…

Mathematically we now know that infinite cannot be described, and this was known even in the ancient Vedic era, and that is why Hinduism accepts all religions, prophets, ideas of God, as true attempts to realize the infinitude of divinity!

6. How to purify our Mind must be the focus of Academia and Religions! Only when a mind has been purified and expanded to have love for all equally thru’ practice of righteousness and commitment to duties for a long period that mind becomes calm and peaceful and only a still mind can allow the infinite Truth of Kingdom of God within and all around, to be experienced. Truth cannot be experienced thru thoughts and intellect; it can be felt when mind is silence, free of all beliefs, thoughts, desires!
