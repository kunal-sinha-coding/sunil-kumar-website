India to Bharat · Essay #100

Without defining the word God can we deny or accept its existence

Modern society has thrown away religions from the mainstream; we think it would be foolish to believe in an imagined and unseen God which was the belief of our primitive forefathers who were no better than savages! Of course, many of the leading men and scientists, as well as Preachers, do believe in God, though they have not bothered to logically establish the existence or otherwise of God.

It should be obvious that mere ‘belief’ in God, without clarity on the idea of God, we cannot agree on a common Goal, a universal idea and ideal of the purpose of Creation and our common Goal – GOD; nor can we accept or deny the existence of God. Let’s try to understand the idea of God as discussed in ancient times, by our so-called ‘savage forefathers’.

2. Everyone knows what an ‘inert’ body or ‘matter’ is. Its definition is clear and is the same in modern science as it is in the most ancient scriptures of mankind, the Vedas. According to modern science an inert body, as only ‘recently’ defined by Newton, is one which continues to stay in its state of rest or of uniform motion along a straight line unless acted upon by a ‘force’, an agency external to it.

Now let’s see how the so called ‘savages’ have scientifically explored, examined and logically presented the idea of an unseen God. An ‘inert’ body, according to the ancients is a non-sentient entity, that has no free will of its own and is necessarily governed by an external agency.

The ancients argued that a stone doesn’t move on its own; it must be moved by an external agency. So, a stone is ‘inert’ and for its motion an external ‘force’ is essential. What is the nature of this ‘force’? This ‘force’ too is inert, because its point of application and its magnitude or its direction is not determined by the ‘force’ itself! The ‘force’ itself doesn’t decide all these issues; they’re determined by the will power of a ‘Conscious living being, say a boy who kicks the stone!

All ‘force’ in science is called Horse-Power because intuitively science also feels that force doesn’t have independent will power because it is inert; and so, a conscious living being, a ‘horse’!

The ancients examined this idea of inert ‘force’ and argued that the root cause for motion of a stone, inert body, is one endowed with sentience, consciousness, as is the boy who is not inert but a conscious living being, jivaatman. The body of the boy is also inert, but the boy is independent of the inert body and the body depends on the boy for its motion or change in its state of motion! The boy thus seemingly has free will of its own. However, the body itself, we know, is inert.

So, the inference is that an inert body becomes a living being only because of, and if it is enlivened by Life, Kingdom of God, Rooha, Spirit, Atman, Self, individual Soul or the embodied Soul, which is of the nature of pure Consciousness, sentience. It is this Consciousness that in association with our mind makes the inert body, a living-being with a free will. The principle or the conclusion that we can now arrive at is that the Root Cause behind any action in an ‘inert body’ is a ‘free-willed-conscious-soul’, a Consciousness, an Intelligence.

So, wherever we see motion, changes, actions we can conclude that behind it must be Intelligence; the ultimate cause must be a conscious principle – and the Shastras term it a ‘god’.

This ‘god-intelligence’ may not be visible, but certainly ‘is’; it exists! It is unreasonable to insist on direct visibility, to be convinced of existence of something. And there is no rule that everything that exists must be perceptible to the senses. None can see the intelligence of say a scientist, but it doesn’t mean it doesn’t exist. Moreover, just because something is perceptible, it doesn’t mean it exists – just as a mirage doesn’t exist!

Thus, we have to accept logical necessity of existence of ‘god’, an intelligence behind every natural phenomenon e.g., Inert Air blows from one direction to another; this may be due to pressure difference or there could be another cause for difference but the ultimate cause cannot be inert, it must be an intelligence associated with free-will, a conscious principle or intelligence, a ‘god’. The shastras call it the wind god. It isn’t visible but has to necessarily exist. Similarly, Earth is inert but is in rotation. The ultimate cause of its rotation must be another conscious intelligence/soul, a presiding deity, we may call earth-god. It isn’t visible but exists. The universe itself is ever-changing and the Conscious Intelligence behind it all is the One Supreme God, the Conscious creator, called by whatever name.

Today science can create rains from clouds with their intelligence; when scientists don’t do it, but it rains naturally, can we not infer that a god-Intelligence is responsible for it.

The relationship between ‘Intelligence-soul’ and ‘inert-mind-body’ was inferred, explored, experienced and discovered by the ancient, who couldn’t be savages but had to be more advanced than all of us today! It is on this ‘connection’ that all religions are based when they talk of ‘ethical/righteous conduct, love, peace, non-killing, truthfulness’; ‘love thy neighbor as thyself’; ‘know thyself’; etc.

Non-acceptance of this god-intelligence behind all actions must be considered as the greatest foolishness of modern age. Who is responsible for modern scientists turning away from God? Perhaps, the immature parrot-like preachers who remain stuck on mere ‘belief’ and preach without growing to realize and experience the spiritual essence of God Consciousness in self and in all Creation.
